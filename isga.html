International Student Guide Amsterdam

Assignment

The students that take the minor International Trade Publishing, Books & More, created a guide book for international students that are here to study. In this guide, the students can get a lot of information and more about Amsterdam. This guide is a physical book, so your job is to design a digital companion for these international students.

Design a webapplication for small screens (eg. smartphone) that gives usefull information from the book. Work your design into a working prototype.

Project: Isga

Date: September 10, 2017

Target group: International Students
